Horse Back Riding


Our Horseback riding classes are such a treat for young adults, and adults alike. We bring you out on some of our pristene horses, who are groomed properly, only stink slightly, and enjoy a brisk run or two during our trips. If you're looking to get back into touch with nature, via a four legged domesticated beast; Look no further!
    Things to consider:
  • Trip Length: 4 hours
  • Skill level: Low, the Horse does all the work!
  • Cost: $250 for a party of 2
  • Guide: Carl
